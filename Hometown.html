I am born and brought up in Kurukshetra, one of the famous places of India.



"Kurukshetra" also known as "Land of Mahabharta" is a renowned historical place in Haryana state of India.The great war of Mahabharta was fought here.Kurukshetra is the second word in the most popular of Indian scriptures – Bhagwad Gita. It is a place that we associate with the Mahabharat war between Kauravas and Pandavas. Kurukshetra is a region named after King Kuru, the ancestor of Kauravas and Pandavas in the Kuru kingdom.
